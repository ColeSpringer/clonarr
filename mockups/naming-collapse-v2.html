Why use a naming scheme?
Adding non-recoverable information (quality source, release group, edition, repack status) to filenames is strongly recommended. It prevents duplicate downloads, stops download loops on re-import, and ensures Radarr always knows what you already have.
IMDb vs TMDb
Both IMDb and TMDb IDs are unique. TMDb can occasionally remove IDs entirely, sometimes re-adding them with a new ID. However, TMDb is more aligned with Radarr since it uses TMDb as its metadata source. IMDb IDs, once present, are very accurate and rarely change.
